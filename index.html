Our brand showcases how Envoy is changing the way people look at workplaces. Ofﬁces are designed to be controlled. But despite our best efforts, humans customize them, and humans are messy. Inspired by the delight in humanity, our focus is to embrace chaos, empower the individual to stand out—and most importantly—bring life to work.

By abstracting the human form and layering those elements over a structured foundation, we create a dynamic composition, emitting confidence, energy, and flexibility.

This guide was designed as a resource and tool kit for our brand. It’s a living document that will continue to evolve as our system grows. Don’t hesitate to reach out with any questions.
